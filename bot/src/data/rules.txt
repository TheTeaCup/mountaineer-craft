Welcome to Mountaineer Craft! To keep things fun and fair for everyone, please follow these rules:

1. **Be respectful**  
   Treat all members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination will not be tolerated.

2. **No spam or flooding**  
   Avoid excessive messages, emojis, caps, or repeated content.

3. **Keep it appropriate**  
   No NSFW, offensive, or inappropriate content.

4. **Follow Discord ToS**  
   All users must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

5. **No cheating or hacking**  
   This includes mods/clients that give unfair advantages (x-ray, fly hacks, etc.).

6. **No exploiting bugs**  
   If you find a bug, report it—don’t abuse it.

7. **Respect builds and property**  
   No griefing, stealing, or destroying other players’ work.

8. **No lag machines or server abuse**  
   Avoid anything that intentionally causes server lag or crashes.

9. **Use channels correctly**  
   Keep discussions in the appropriate channels.

10. **No advertising**  
   Do not promote other servers, services, or social media without permission.

*Breaking rules may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans depending on severity.*
